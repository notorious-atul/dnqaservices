Types of Testing

  • Functional Testing: Ensuring that each function of the software application operates according to the specified requirements.
  • Performance Testing: Evaluating the responsiveness and stability of the software under varying workloads.
  • Security Testing: Identifying vulnerabilities and ensuring that the software is secure against unauthorized access and attacks.
  • Usability Testing: Assessing the user-friendliness and overall user experience of the software.

Testing Methodology

We follow an agile testing methodology, integrating testing processes seamlessly into the development lifecycle. This ensures early detection of issues and allows for rapid and effective resolution.

SDET Services
